Our owner James has always had a passion for ingredients and a love of Pizza. He only uses the freshest ingredients and all our pizza are cooked in a wood burning stove. The ingredients are also all locally sources and of the higher quility. James moved to Salt Lake City from his home just outside New York and has brought the traditions he learned there from his family run pizza house with its 100 year history. We make any may of our products in house as we can to make sure we are bringing you the freshest and best product we possible can, this is a true passion for us and we hope you will see that in our final product.
